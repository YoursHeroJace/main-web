About Aerokleaners

Founded by students, Aerokleaners focuses on practical environmental actions combined with awareness campaigns in schools. Our programs are low-cost, high-impact, and replicable.

Education

Workshops and classroom talks designed for students (age 10–18) to teach waste segregation and sustainable habits.

Clean-up Drives

Organized, safe drives focusing on parks, school campuses, and local streets where students can participate under supervision.

Green Projects

Tree plantation, composting pits, and small urban gardening projects to improve biodiversity on campus.

Why join?

Hands-on experience, leadership opportunities, certificates for volunteers, and measurable improvements in local environment.

  • Learn teamwork & leadership
  • Earn volunteer hours
  • Make your school greener

Recent Projects

Beach Cleanup — August 2025

Tree Planting Drive — March 2025

We planted 120 saplings in collaboration with school environmental club and local nursery.

Composting Workshop — May 2025

Students learned to build low-cost compost pits and kitchen waste segregation for school canteens.
